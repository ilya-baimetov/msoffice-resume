Features

Set it and forget it

Office Resume works silently in the background. Your documents come back exactly where you left them.

Auto Session Restore

Documents reopen automatically when Word, Excel, or PowerPoint relaunches. No manual intervention needed.

Background Operation

Runs as a login helper daemon. Starts automatically at login, captures snapshots silently, uses minimal resources.

Unsaved Doc Protection

Detects untitled and unsaved documents and force-saves them to temporary storage. Never lose an unsaved draft again.

Menu Bar Controls

Pause tracking, trigger an immediate restore, or clear the current session — all from a simple menu bar dropdown.

Privacy First

Everything stays on your Mac. No document content is uploaded. No analytics or telemetry. Fully local operation.

Cross-App Support

Works across Word, Excel, and PowerPoint. Outlook relaunch support included. OneNote planned for a future release.
